CSV Format

Your CSV must have these column headers:

question_text, question_type, options, correct_answer, explanation, marks, difficulty, subject, topic, tags, status
  • options: Separate with | (pipe)
  • correct_answer: Separate with |
  • tags: Separate with |
  • subject: Use the Subject UUID
  • question_type: mcq, true_false, fill_blank, essay, matching, ordering, image
  • difficulty: easy, medium, hard
